Hi, I'm Chloe Oriotis, a mechanical engineer from the University of Toronto. I specialized in mechatronics and bioengineering and received a certificate in communication, and I've worked in the medical device field, aerospace, automotive, and education. I'm passionate about leadership development, design engineering and lifelong learning. Before I entered university I was a lifeguard for the City of Toronto and a camp counsellor for little kiddos - this is where I learned a strong work ethic is crucial to success.

This website isn't just a portfolio, it's a project I took on to teach myself a new skill: web development. I learned HTML and CSS to build the site and GitHub to bring it to life online. Turns out web dev is really fun! I have some less nerdy hobbies too - in the summer you can find me biking the ravine trails around Toronto or doing laps around the pool. I also have a black belt in karate, which I hope to get back into soon. Paleontology has fascinated me since I was little and I love collecting fossils. If you're looking for a new favourite dinosaur, check out the troodon formosus - super smart, absolutely adorable.
